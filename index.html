Rain season

Usually happens between April and September. As winter ends, warm, moist air from the southwest Indian Ocean blows toward countries like India, Sri Lanka, Bangladesh, and Myanmar. The summer monsoon brings a humid climate and torrential rainfall to these areas.

Mansoon season

This word monsoon has been derived from an Arabic word 'Mausim'. The meaning of mausim is season. The seasonal wind movement which brings rain and precipitation is described by the monsoon. Seasonal shift in the wind's direction is called monsoon.
